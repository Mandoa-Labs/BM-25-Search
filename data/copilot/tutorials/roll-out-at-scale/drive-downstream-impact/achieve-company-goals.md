# Achieving your company's engineering goals with GitHub Copilot

Plan your rollout based on GitHub's recommended approach for driving and measuring improvements.

When your company rolls out a new tool such as GitHub Copilot, you will want to measure the impact of the tool on your engineering systems and assess the tool's contribution to your company's goals.

GitHub's [Engineering System Success Playbook](https://resources.github.com/engineering-system-success-playbook/) (ESSP) provides strategies and metrics for driving meaningful and measurable improvements. The playbook recommends a three-step process for solving engineering challenges:

1. Identify the current barriers to success.
2. Evaluate what needs to be done to achieve your goals.
3. Implement your changes, monitor results, and adjust.
